how it works

Three things happen, in order

There's no sign-up flow to design around — just a key you hold, a place you're from, and a way to be found.

01

Pick a handle

Choose a name. Behind it, a nostr keypair is generated and held only by you — there's no password of ours to leak, because we never have one.

02

Get discovered nearby

Set your country and language once. Anyone nearby can see that you're around and willing to help, without you exposing anything else about yourself.

03

Give or ask for help

Leave someone a message directly, or post to the open forum — visible to your local area, your region, or the whole world, your choice. Every post is signed by nostr, not stored by us.

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know.

What is a nostr keypair?+
A cryptographic key pair — a private key (secret) and a public key (handle). Your identity is yours alone; we never see your private key.
Is this free?+
Yes. This is a solo, unfunded project built in public. If it's useful, donations help keep it alive.
How do I reset my password?+
Use the "Forgot password" option in the sign-in view. A reset link will be sent to your email.
What data do you store?+
Nothing server-side. Your handle, language, and presence live in your nostr identity. We only store consent preferences locally in your browser.
Can I change my handle later?+
Not yet — handles are tied to the nostr keypair generated on sign-up. A migration feature is planned.
